My Journey in Design

At the age of 14, I sketched construction machinery for JCB Equipments India, earning praise from their leadership. Their encouragement fueled my passion for design, leading me to pursue a career focused on solving usability issues across various objects.

At work, I like to shape strategic decisions of stakeholders to transform virtual data concepts into useful, simple, and beautiful visual elements.

I am also keen on working with design systems, as it helps in developing the product growth and development. I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics — it's about creating systems that empower teams and scale with the business.
